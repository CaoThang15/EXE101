Terms & Conditions for Child Care Services

1. Enrollment & Eligibility

Our child care services are available for children aged 6 months to 6 years. Parents must complete the registration process, including providing medical records, emergency contact details, and signed consent forms before enrollment.


2. Health & Safety Policies

We prioritize the well-being of all children by maintaining strict hygiene standards, regular health check-ups, and immediate medical attention in case of emergencies. Sick children must remain at home until fully recovered.


3. Daily Routines & Activities

Our structured programs include educational activities, supervised playtime, healthy meals, and rest periods. Parents are encouraged to provide any special instructions regarding their child's dietary or medical needs.


4. Payment & Cancellation Policy

Fees must be paid in advance on a monthly basis. Cancellations require at least a 7-day notice for a partial refund. No refunds are issued for absences due to illness or personal reasons.


5. Code of Conduct & Parental Responsibilities

We foster a respectful and nurturing environment for all children. Parents must adhere to drop-off and pick-up schedules and ensure their child follows basic behavior guidelines. Any concerns should be communicated to our staff promptly.
